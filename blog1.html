My Summer Internship at Microchip Technology Inc.!

I finished my internship at Microchip Technology Inc. last Friday! It was an extremely rewarding summer spent gaining real-world experience working in Silicon Valley.

I have been blessed with a wealth of knowledge and experience that I can attribute to the projects and people I worked with. I authored a published application note on NTP Time Server Configuration for their industry-leading, ultra-precise time synchronization device, which is available on the Microchip website (linked below). Alongside this, I applied my academic knowledge to software development projects; from writing automated testing phases to ensure firmware image validity to enhancing data visualization software used by the company, 'Time Monitor Analyzer.' I am grateful to the inspiring and dedicated software developers, Eric Ivanov and Lee Cosart, for their guidance on these projects.

I'd like to thank my mentor, Paul Skoog, for all he taught me, and my manager, Barry Dropping, for his continuous support. :) Special thanks to Brianna Casarez for her work on the HR front! This internship has been pivotal for my transition from student to professional software engineer in the coming year.
