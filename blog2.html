Attending the Grace Hopper Celebration 2023!

This school year I had the amazing opportunity the attend the Grace Hopper Celebration in Orlando! I'm so grateful to have been chose to go - I got to meet so many amazing female and non binary people and learn about their passion and excitement for tech.

I felt so empowered by seeing all these hardworking and influential women and non binary people gathered together! Including our voices in the future of tech is #thewayforward.
