Starting as a Software Dev @ Hack4Impact

This past month I began my role as a software developer for Hack4Impact Cal Poly.

As a computer science student, I have always wanted to utilize my programming skills for social good. Nowadays, software is embedded within our society and daily lives; any opportunity to create software that is useful and impactful is something I am proud to take advantage of.

As a software developer at Hack4Impact, I will work alongside a team of developers, project managers, and designers, to develop a software project for a local non-profit in San Luis Obispo county.

I plan to update my blog with more posts as we begin development on our project!
